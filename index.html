About

I am Rafid Umayer Murshed, a first-year Computer Science PhD student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I completed an M.Sc. (Thesis) in Electrical Engineering at The University of Texas at Dallas and a B.Sc. in Electrical & Electronic Engineering at BUET.

  • Interests Physics-informed AI • RF learning • UM-MIMO beamforming • RIS/Metasurfaces • Neural fields
  • Goal Build trustworthy wireless digital twins for 6G.

Research Highlights

Robust UM-MIMO under imperfect CSI

Self-supervised contrastive learning and physics-aware augmentations to improve achievable rate and error resilience.

Neural RF fields

NeRF/GS-style representations adapted for multi-frequency channel estimation and propagation modeling.
